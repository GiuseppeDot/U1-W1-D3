Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberriers and Brie

Wow your taste with this festival focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberrier, and creamy bride. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
    • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinty sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bier, cut into cubes (scrap rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberrier

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing blow, or in a stand mixer with paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the blow when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
